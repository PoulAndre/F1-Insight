About this page

  • The content

    The main point of this website is to be able to inform YOU as quickly as possible about a race. There are no live updates during a race, but are updated during the 2026 season. I have tested a few different APIs.

    For the 2026 season itself, I have created my own API by following Formula 1's schedule. For other things, such as the points system for the drivers and for the teams, I have used OpenF1's API (NB: it is in beta - incorrect points may occur).

    To show the weather conditions on the tracks, I have used OpenWeather. Here I have tested a few different things and fetch weather data for the city from my own API.

  • Design info

    I have chosen this layout to provide an overview of the 2026 Formula 1 season - race by race. The content should be easy to read and understand, the nav is highly visible to navigate easier, and the page has no scroll function that allows the user to have everthing right in front of them!

    I chose to use the choice of colors because it reminded me of Formula 1. They fit the theme best and complement each other well.
